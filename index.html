About Me

Welcome! My name is Sophia and I am front-end developer in the Silicon Valley and recent graduate of Coding Dojo, an intensive programming boot camp.

During my time at the Dojo, I learned three full stacks--Python, PHP, and MEAN--and created several projects.

I am passionate about technology, innovation, and finding new approaches to everyday problems. I strive to integrate my Psychology and Marketing background into projects to provide users with an experience that will enrich and simplify their lives.

Outside of coding, I volunteer as a mentor for women with eating disorders, co-lead chat support groups, and dance as much as possible!
